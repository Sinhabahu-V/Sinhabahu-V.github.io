## Writing for the nation, not for a person

**Sinhabahu** is an independent platform for clear thinking about Sri Lanka — its history, its security, its place among nations, and the decisions that will shape its future.

This is not a platform built around any person, party, or ideology. It is built around the nation. The writing here will praise where praise is due and criticise where criticism is necessary, regardless of who holds power.

## What you will find here

The essays here cover geopolitics and great power competition as they bear on Sri Lanka and the Indian Ocean; national resilience and what it truly means to build a state capable of withstanding external pressure and internal decay; history as an indispensable tool for understanding the present; and strategy as a discipline for those who must think clearly before they act.

## Why Sinhabahu

The name reaches back to the founding myth of the Sinhala people — a warrior-king who chose duty over comfort, whose deliberate choices set a civilisation in motion. That spirit of purposeful action, of thinking before speaking and acting with consequence, is what this platform aspires to carry forward.
